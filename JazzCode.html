JAZZ

HISTORY

Jazz started in the late 19th and the early 20th centuries and developed from the blues and ragtime.
It is seen as "Americas classical music and is mostly improvised on the spot during performances which
requires a lot of skill but most importantly jazz is very rhythmic with a lot of "swing" and "bent" motions.
Each musician has their own sound and style which means you can listen to a dozen of recordings of the
same song but they will all sound different, so they can take a familiar song and create something fresh
but will have a tune that everyone knows into something personal for the artist. Also not all artists will
sing in their music, some will just play their instruments. Those are just some reasons why jazz
is a great form of art and why it is considered "America's classical music".
