HISTORY

Nothing can compare to rap music, it is one of the most popular music genres. The roots of rap is planted deep in the heart of Africa. The first DJ also known as the founding father of hip hop goes by the name DJ Kool Herc. He introduced us to rap on August 11th, 1973 in the Bronx, New York by hosting back to school parties. POPULAR ARTIST

Nicki Minaj

Nicki Minaj's first solo album, Pink Friday, peaked at No. 1 on the Billboard 200. The album spawned five Top 40 singles: "Your Love," "Check It Out," "Right Thru Me," "Moment 4 Life," and "Fly."

Kendrick Lamar

Kendrick Lamar broke through with his 2012 album, Good Kid, M.A.A.D City, which peaked at No. 2 on the Billboard 200 and No. 1 on Billboard's Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums chart
